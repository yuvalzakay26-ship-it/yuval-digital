# Services — What yuval.digital Builds

The current service catalog. Each service is a distinct, scoped offer with a defined outcome. The list is intentionally short and honest — only services Yuval can genuinely deliver well today.

---

## 1. Business Websites

**Outcome:** A custom website that signals seriousness, conveys the offer clearly, and grows with the business.

**Includes**
- Discovery: positioning, audience, key actions.
- Bilingual content architecture (HE / EN), with RTL/LTR symmetry.
- Custom design system: type scale, color, motion, components.
- Hand-coded React + Vite frontend, accessible and SEO-ready.
- Smart contact and lead forms.
- Documented launch and a short walkthrough for the team.

---

## 2. Landing Pages

**Outcome:** A single focused page that converts campaign or referral traffic into qualified leads.

**Includes**
- Conversion-led copy structure.
- One clear primary action; secondary CTAs only where they help.
- Smart form with validation and routing to email / CRM / WhatsApp.
- Fast load, mobile-first, A/B-ready structure.

---

## 3. Smart Automation Flows

**Outcome:** Hours of recurring manual work eliminated, with fewer human errors.

**Includes**
- Workflow audit: what's manual, what's slow, what's error-prone.
- Automation build with Make, n8n, Zapier, or small custom Node services where appropriate.
- API integration across CRM, email, billing, WhatsApp, calendars, and forms.
- Live alerts where signal matters.

---

## 4. AI-Assisted Content Systems

**Outcome:** AI workflows embedded into the content process — drafting, translating, summarizing, generating variants — tuned to the brand voice.

**Includes**
- Prompt design for the brand voice and brief.
- Bilingual HE/EN flows with consistent quality.
- Connections to existing content tools and CMS.
- Operational runbook so the client's team can use it.

This is *AI used as a tool*, not "AI engineering" or model training.

---

## 5. Internal Business Tools

**Outcome:** Lightweight internal screens, dashboards and forms that help your team work faster and with fewer mistakes.

**Includes**
- Domain modeling for the team's day-to-day.
- React frontend + simple backend or serverless when needed.
- Basic authentication and permissions.
- Reports and exports the operations team can use.

---

## 6. Digital Presence Upgrades

**Outcome:** Refreshing what already exists rather than rebuilding from zero — cheaper, faster, and often enough.

**Includes**
- Mini-redesign of the existing site (type, color, components).
- Mobile experience fixes.
- Speed and Core Web Vitals improvements.
- Form tuning, CTA placement, content polish.

---

## What Is *Not* Offered

- Templated multi-page sites with no customization.
- Mobile native apps (referred to a trusted partner when needed).
- Pure SEO content services.
- AI model research, fine-tuning, or training.
- "Years of legacy" engagements — we are honest about being early.

## Bundling

Services can be combined for clients buying a complete digital improvement:

- **Starter Pack:** Business website + lead form automation.
- **Campaign Pack:** Landing page + AI-assisted content system + WhatsApp routing.
- **Refresh Pack:** Digital presence upgrade + automation cleanup.
