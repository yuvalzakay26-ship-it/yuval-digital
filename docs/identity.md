# Identity — Who Yuval Is

## One-line

Yuval is an outstanding practical software graduate, a hands-on digital builder, and a smart user of modern AI tools — operating as a focused one-builder studio under the `yuval.digital` brand.

## Professional Identity

Yuval is presented honestly through three layered identities, broadest to most specific:

1. **Practical software graduate (with honors).** A strong technical foundation in engineering thinking, system design and problem-solving.
2. **Hands-on digital builder.** Builds websites, landing pages, automations, internal tools and AI-assisted content systems — end to end.
3. **Smart user of AI tools.** Uses modern AI as part of the daily workflow to build faster, think more clearly, and deliver more polished results. *Not* an AI researcher, *not* an "AI engineer" — a builder who uses AI well.

## Mental Model

> "Yuval thinks like an engineer, designs with care, and builds with AI tools as part of his everyday flow."

Three ingrained postures:

- **Engineering posture.** Clean code, sensible architecture, problem-first thinking.
- **Craft posture.** Spacing, type, motion and finish are non-negotiable. Premium feel comes from the small details.
- **Honest posture.** Says what is real, labels what is concept, only promises what can be delivered.

## Strengths (real)

- Solid technical foundation from a practical software engineering program.
- Strong taste for product, design and brand.
- Effective with modern AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Copilot, etc.) as everyday building partners.
- Bilingual native delivery in Hebrew and English.
- Direct communication and full personal attention per project.
- Highly motivated to prove the work — every project is treated as a portfolio piece.

## What Yuval Is *Not*

- Not a "Software Engineer" with years of industry experience.
- Not an "AI Engineer" or AI researcher.
- Not a fake agency front with subcontracted backend.
- Not someone who has shipped dozens of paying client systems — yet.

This is honesty, not modesty. The work itself is the proof.

## How Yuval Works With Clients

1. **Listen.** First conversation is mostly the client's words; Yuval extracts the real problem.
2. **Frame.** A short written framing of goals, constraints, and the system shape — agreed before any code.
3. **Build.** Live previews, weekly visible progress, no black-box delivery.
4. **Hand over.** Documented, owned, and operable by the client's team.
5. **Stay close.** Ongoing fixes and improvements as the business grows.

## Public Voice (first-person samples)

- "I'm at the start of my path as an independent builder — and that's exactly why your project will get my full attention."
- "I use AI as a working tool every day. Not as a sales pitch."
- "I'd rather build less and finish it excellently."
- "If something is a concept, I'll mark it as a concept."
